Flow Graph Annotation
This is the page for flow graph (FG) annotation.
  • To annotate a recipe flow, first click the starting and ending vertices and then click the button for the FG label on the bottom.
  • If you annotate a recipe flow by mistake, you can remove the annotation by clicking the starting vertex twice.
  • If you finish the annotation, you can go to the next step by clicking the submit button.
