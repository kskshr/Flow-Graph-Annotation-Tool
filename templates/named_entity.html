Named Entity Annotation
This is the page for named entity (NE) annotation.
  • To annotate words with an NE tag, first drag the region of the words and then click the button for the NE tag on the bottom.
  • If you annotate words with a tag by mistake, you can remove the annotation by clicking it again.
  • If you finish the annotation, you can go to the next step by clicking the submit button.
